About Me

I’m a self-taught web developer based in Paris, with a diploma in audio production and a passion for creating lively, user-friendly websites. After years behind the mixing desk, I discovered my true calling building digital experiences. I specialize in crafting modern, responsive sites for hospitality venues—cafés, bars, and restaurants— that not only look great but also help bring in more customers.

From wireframes to final launch, I handle everything: responsive layouts, performance optimization, and intuitive content management. Whether you need a sleek menu page, an events calendar, or an easy-to-use reservation form, I’ve got you covered.
