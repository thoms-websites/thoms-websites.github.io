About Me

I discovered my passion for web development in 2020 and taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by building sites for friends and local cafés. Since then I’ve honed my skills in responsive design, performance optimization, and modern tooling (Git, npm, build pipelines). I love the challenge of turning a business’s vision into a pixel-perfect, user-friendly website that not only looks great but also drives real results.

When I’m not coding you’ll find me experimenting with CSS art, exploring new JavaScript frameworks, or sampling lattes at the Corner Café down the street. Let’s partner up to give your venue a website that truly reflects its personality and keeps customers coming back.
